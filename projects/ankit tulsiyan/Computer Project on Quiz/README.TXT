PROJECT TITLE: Bournvita Quiz Contest
PURPOSE OF PROJECT: This program consists of two main fields BQC Quiz Contest for single player and Inter School quiz Contest 
                    which is played between Two Teams.In BQC Quiz Contest 10 Questions are there in each category and the 
                    user has to score more than 40 marks to qualify for the All-Asia BQC Finals.Whereas in Inter School Quiz 
                    Contest there are 6 six rounds each having a question for the two teams.The team with maximum points at 
                    the end of the program wins the game.This program helps you to test your General Knowledge and also helps 
                    in winning a BQC Merit Certificate .
